Bears vs. Art Soft Launch
Frequently Asked Questions

Hey guys!

We've just unveiled Bears vs. Art to the world and everyone at Halfbrick is buzzing with excitement. The debut trailer is up on YouTube and many of you are clearly itching to know more!

We'll be posting more details over the coming weeks, but for now we want to cover the game's soft launch in as much detail as possible. This will ensure everyone knows exactly what to expect moving forward.

  • Why is Bears vs. Art only available in certain regions?

    Bears vs. Art supports a range of new technology, making it crucial to test these systems before millions of people download the game. The best and most common solution to this is a staggered release aka 'soft launch'.

    Soft launches allow us to alter gameplay without inconveniencing a large number of players should something go wrong. Remember, Bears vs. Art is Halfbrick's first true puzzle game and we want it to be amazing!

    Permalink

  • Will you be making any adjustments during the soft launch?

    Yes, all players should expect minor changes before we launch the game globally. Variables such as pricing, move limits or even level designs could be affected so don't be alarmed if you notice this happening. If in doubt, shoot us an email from within the game and we should be able to help!

  • I found a problem with the game! How do I submit feedback?

    Contacting the developers of Bears vs. Art is easy. Here's how you do it!

    1. Access the game's main menu by either:
      1. Closing and opening the game, or
      2. Tapping the 'back' arrow in the bottom left corner of the map
    2. Click Connect once you reach the main splash screen. This should open the My Profile box.
    3. Tap Support in the bottom right corner of the My Profile box.
    4. Write your feedback in as much detail as possible and hit Send
