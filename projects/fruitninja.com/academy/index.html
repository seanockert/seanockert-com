Addition & Subtraction

Players must solve a sequence of addition or subtraction equations by picking the correct number of bananas and feeding them to the monkey statue.

Addition game covers numbers 1 to 9, with a maximum sum of 18.

Subtraction game covers numbers 0 to 9, with all answers less than 10.

Multiplication (skip counting)

Players must complete multiplication equations using the skip counting method. Boxes contain a quantity of fruit. Add the number of fruit boxes needed to complete the equation. Juice the fruit to see if the answer is correct.

Count by multiple of 2. Min/Max answer of 2 to 10

Count by multiple of 3. Min/Max answer of 3 to 15

Count by multiple of 5. Min/Max answer of 5 to 25

Shapes & Fractions

Players learn to recognise shapes by slicing the correct shapes with their finger.

Shape recognition for square, triangle, circle, oval, diamond, hexagon and trapezoids.

This game mode also covers fractions for wholes, thirds, halves and quarters.
